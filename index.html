About Me

An experienced consultant with over 25 years of Business Intelligence reporting, IT service
and Dashboard presentation.
On leaving college, I started my career in accountancy working for a Porsche car garage.
This enabled me to get the financial experience needed for starting in the Financial Reporting
world installing, configuring and supporting an application entitled 'F9'.
My IT skills came to fruition at Vestas Wind Systems (a global alternative energy company
providing wind turbines to the energy industry) where I was consulting and eventually
employed to help with an onsite presence for the Finance Director, who was using 'F9'.
I was also part of the IT department who funded the team to become fully Microsoft Certified
Technicians. My qualified speciality area was server and security.
From here I progressed into running another software application company called
Infineo Solutions, a French owned provider in the BI Reporting sector. The software was
recently sold to Sage and I have purchased the UK office, of which I was the director, along
with all of the clients and the VARs attached to the company.
I have now diversified Infineo Solutions Ltd's portfolio into providing web design,
SQL programming and Excel training.
